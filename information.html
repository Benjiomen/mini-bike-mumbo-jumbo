when were mini bikes popular The late 1960s to the early 1970s was the height of the minibike/go-kart era. Minibike clubs and shows are still popular today. Minibikes were even used during the wars. Paratroopers would jump out of planes with them on their back, and when they got to the ground they would just hop on the bike and take off.
